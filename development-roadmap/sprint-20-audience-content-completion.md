## Content Templates

### Plain English Template (Everyday)
```markdown
## [Milestone Title]

### What Happened
[2-3 sentences. No jargon. Simple subject-verb-object sentences.]

### Think of It Like...
[Analogy from everyday life. Reference experiences from 1950s-1990s if possible.
Examples: library card catalogs, telephone operators, TV channel dials]

### How This Affects You
[Bullet points connecting to:]
- Healthcare / doctors
- Shopping / online
- Family / grandchildren
- Daily technology use
- News / information

### Try It Yourself (Optional)
[Safe, specific activity with exact steps]
1. Go to [specific URL]
2. Click [specific button]
3. Type [specific example]
4. Notice [specific thing]

### Watch Out For
[Bullet points on:]
- Scam awareness
- Misinformation
- Privacy concerns
- When NOT to trust AI
```

### Executive Brief Template (Leaders)
```markdown
## [Milestone Title]

### The Bottom Line
[1-2 sentences. What a CEO needs to know in 15 seconds.]

### Business Implications
[Strategic analysis:]
- Market impact
- Competitive dynamics
- Cost/efficiency implications
- Revenue opportunities
- Risk factors

### Questions to Ask Your Team
1. [Diagnostic question]
2. [Capability question]
3. [Competitive question]
4. [Resource question]

### Competitor Watch
[What sophisticated competitors are likely doing]

### Action Items
- [ ] [Specific action with owner]
- [ ] [Specific action with timeline]
- [ ] [Specific action with deliverable]

### Further Reading
- [HBR/McKinsey article link]
- [Industry report link]
```

## Post-Sprint: User Testing Plan

### Testing Protocol
1. **Recruit**: 3 users per audience (6 total)
2. **Environment**: Their own device, natural setting
3. **Task**: Complete assigned path
4. **Observe**: Where they pause, struggle, smile
5. **Interview**: 15-min post-completion questions
6. **Iterate**: Prioritize fixes for Sprint 21

### Key Questions
- Where did you get confused?
- What did you wish was explained better?
- Would you recommend this to [friend/colleague]?
- What would you do differently now?
- What's missing?
